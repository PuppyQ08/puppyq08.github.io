Spatiotemporal Modeling of Single-Cell Trajectories with Neural ODEs

Jane Doe, Carlos Rivera, John Smith

TrajODE introduces a neural ordinary differential equation framework for learning continuous-time cell state trajectories from snapshot single-cell RNA-seq data. The model recovers biologically coherent lineage relationships while quantifying transcriptional velocity at single-cell resolution.

Equivariant Graph Neural Networks for Molecular Property Prediction

Jane Doe, Wei Zhang, John Smith

We present EquivMol, an SE(3)-equivariant message-passing network that models molecular geometry directly in 3D space. Evaluated across QM9 and MD17 benchmarks, EquivMol achieves state-of-the-art performance with significantly lower sample complexity than competing architectures.
